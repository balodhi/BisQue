Description:

This module allows to manually annotate an image and save the corresponding mask in your Bisque images directory.

How to run the module:

  • Select data for processing:
    • You can load an image from your account or among the ones publicly available on the Bisque server.
    • You can upload your own image.

  • Draw polygons on the object(s) of interest. How to draw a ploygon:
    • From the annotations toolbar on the image, click on polygon button.
    • Click on the image where you want the polygon vertices to be.
    • Finish the polyline by double-clicking the last vertex.
    • Repeat all the previous steps until all the desired polygons are drawn on the image.

  • Run:
    • Click on the "Run" button to execute the module.

  • Results:
    • If the module run successfully, a summary table will be displayed. The mask has been stored to your Bisque images directory.
